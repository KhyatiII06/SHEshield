The Problem

Danger comes from two fronts. Help comes from none of them, together.

Women face threats both physical and digital — but police, cyber cell, and emergency response all operate in silos. The victim is left to navigate each, alone.

47%
of women in India report facing online harassment

83%
of cybercrime cases against women go unreported
0
existing platforms unify physical and cyber safety

Fragmented systems

Police, Cyber Cell, and ERSS 112 all operate in silos — a victim must contact each one separately, repeating the same story every time.

Evidence gets lost

Screenshots get deleted, timestamps go missing, and the chain of custody breaks down long before a case ever reaches court.

No AI protection

Phishing links, fake profiles, and harassment patterns are typically only flagged after the harm has already happened.

Reporting barriers

Complex FIR processes, language barriers, and social stigma keep most women from ever filing a report at all.

Key Features

Six modules. One ecosystem.

Each module works standalone — but together they close the gap between a threat and a response.

Emergency SOS

  • One-touch + voice trigger, SMS fallback
  • Silent panic mode — no screen change
  • Live GPS to 112 and guardians instantly
  • Wearable trigger via Fitbit, Apple Watch

Cybercrime Reporting

  • Stalking, blackmail, fraud — guided forms
  • AI-assisted FIR draft with IPC sections
  • Direct submission to Cyber Crime Branch
  • Multilingual: Hindi, Gujarati, English, Tamil

Evidence Vault

  • AES-256 encryption applied at upload
  • SHA-256 hash + immutable timestamps
  • Chain-of-custody for court admissibility
  • Screenshots, chats, URLs, video, audio

AI Threat Detection

  • Phishing URL scanner with real-time risk score
  • Fake profile impersonation detection
  • Harassment analysis with IPC section mapping
  • Unsafe zone prediction from crime density

Guardian Network

  • Instant SOS push to trusted contacts
  • Live location sharing during incidents
  • Incident status updates in real-time
  • Safe arrival confirmation and zone alerts

Police Dashboard

  • Real-time SOS and cybercrime alert feed
  • Repeat offender tracker across cases
  • Digital evidence with chain-of-custody
  • Crime pattern analytics and heatmaps
